Who we are

The Kalamazoo Arb Census Team consists of three members: Teddy Jacobson, Judah Karesh, and Juniper Pasternak. This web app was created as the team's final project for Dr. Vargas-Pérez’s COMP 484/490, “Computing for the Environment and Social Justice.” It was also made in collaboration with Dr. Girdler, as its purpose is to assist in the ongoing data collection the Environmental Studies department does of the Lillian Anderson Arboretum.
